HealthAdvisor: GenAI-based personalized wellbeing app

GOAL

Effective and personalized wellbeing advisor.

PROBLEM

Mental health crisis among the general population.

SOLUTION

Actionable plans based on knowledge of individualsâ€™ physiological states, behavior, context, personal data, and health goals; leveraging GenAI. The app provides encouraging messages and recommendations to connect, exercise, see a doctor, etc.

HYPOTHESIS

GenAI-based personalized plans can outperform existing one-size-fits-all solutions.

PoC Goal

Build a Proof of Concept (PoC) to generate actionable plans via Llama 2 & augmented context, including individual physiological data, other personal data, and health goals.

PoC Details

  • Use GenAI for generating suggestions and checking the safety of the Intervention.
  • Leverage Llama 2 and tune the prompt.
  • Generate actionable plans/suggestions based on individual diagnosis and intervention suggestions.
  • Assess the mental state using individual physiological conditions, categorized as High, Medium, Low Stress.
  • Input task-based prompts to GenAI to generate suggestions.
  • Support conversational interaction after the initial suggestion.

PoC Stretch Goal

  • Evaluate the perceived effectiveness of GenAI actionable plans against human-generated best practices.
  • Fine-tune the model based on specific sources (e.g., WebMD).
  • Provide more personalized suggestions.
  • Use GenAI for generating suggestions, checking the safety of Intervention, and assessing individual physiological states.
  • Forecast individual physiological state as part of the assessment.
