The Taxman

The goals of our project:

  • Develop an advanced tax filing system that automates the process of filling out 1040N forms using:
    • Optical Character Recognition (OCR)
    • Fine tuned Lllama 2 LLM
  • Develop an AI assisted chatbot on a 70B Lllama 2 LLM to assist on any queries on the terminology and lexicon

We aim to create a user friendly and efficient tool which can make the process of tax filing especially for sole individuals better than existing platforms like SprintTax and TurboTax
